# Governance

Calcaneal Case is intended to be a long-term open-source medical education and case-management project.

## Roles

### Clinical Lead

Owns medical direction, calcaneal fracture teaching quality, three-step reduction method content, and final review of clinical workflows.

### Technical Maintainer

Owns architecture, code quality, releases, security fixes, and development workflow.

### Case Administrator

Reviews de-identification, case quality, visibility level, and teaching suitability.

### Teaching Administrator

Provides private teaching for administrator-visible cases and helps maintain respectful, useful discussion.

### Research Coordinator

Owns multi-center research workflow, ethics approval requirements, data dictionary, and research export review.

## Decision Making

- Privacy, safety, and legal compliance override convenience.
- Clinical logic changes require clinical lead approval.
- Data visibility and identity-review changes require maintainer and clinical review.
- Research workflow changes require research coordinator review.
- Commercial content must be clearly separated from clinical discussion.

## Release Rhythm

- Small fixes: weekly when available
- Product updates: monthly
- Public teaching case updates: quarterly
- International content updates: twice per year

## Maintainer Automation

Codex or similar automation may be used for:

- issue triage
- documentation updates
- regression checks
- changelog drafts
- dependency review
- privacy checklist reminders

Automation must not make final clinical decisions, approve patient data disclosure, or replace human review.
